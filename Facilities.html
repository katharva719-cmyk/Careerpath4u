What Facilities We Give You

1) Free Food

Google provides free, gourmet food for all its employees, known as "Googlers." If a Googler brings their dog to the office, their pet gets the special name "Doogler."

2) Relax House

Designed for unwinding, the "Relax House" is a dedicated space where employees can take a break from work and recharge their minds and bodies.

3) Gym and Medical Health

Your well-being is a priority. Google offers on-site gyms and comprehensive medical facilities to ensure employees are both physically and mentally fit to work happily.

4) Work from Home

Google provides its employees with the flexibility to work from home, allowing for a better work-life balance and a comfortable working environment.

5) Death Benefit

In the unfortunate event of an employee's passing, Google provides significant support to their family. This includes 50% of their salary for a decade and a monthly stipend of $1,000 for each child until they turn 19.

6) Independent Work Culture

At Google, we believe in a no-pressure work environment. Employees are encouraged to work independently, fostering creativity and a positive, autonomous atmosphere.
