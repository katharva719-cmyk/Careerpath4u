Three Ways to Apply for a Google Job

1) Apply Online via Website

You can apply for a job interview at Google by submitting your resume online. If your profile is impressive and meets their requirements, the recruitment team will contact you to schedule an interview at one of their offices.

2) Campus Placement

Google has partnerships with top universities and colleges, such as IIT, JEE, and DTU, to recruit top talent directly. Through campus placements, students from these institutions get the chance to interview for a position with Google.

3) APAC Test

Google also holds an annual APAC (Asia-Pacific) test. In this competition, participants are challenged to design a website. If you can create an impressive and innovative design, you have a strong chance of being offered a job at Googleplex.
