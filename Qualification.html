What Qualifications Do You Need to Get a Job?

1) Bachelor's Degree

You need a B.Tech or MCA degree. A degree is the most important part of the job application process; many cases are rejected due to a lack of a proper degree.

2) Academic Performance

You need a minimum of 65% in your entire academic career (10th, 12th, B.Tech, or MCA). Your grades are important as they demonstrate your talent. If your marks are less than 65%, you will likely be rejected for an interview.

3) English Language Skills

Candidates must have a strong command of the English language. Most interviews are conducted in English, so your language skills and behavior must be professional. It is essential to master your English speaking skills first.

4) Technical Knowledge

You need a full understanding of Internet, Web research, Online advertising, Fraud detection, Numerical analysis, and E-commerce. Your main focus and job will depend on these subjects, so a strong command is crucial.

5) Mathematics Expertise

Mathematics is another critical subject that helps you get a job at Google. Math skills are used for complex calculations and problem-solving, which are essential for many roles.
